About Me

Lingfeng Huang is PhD student of School of Aerospace, Transport and Manufacturing. His research topic is Integration of Motors and Power Electronics for Enhanced Energy Conversion and Management.

The efficient integration of motors and power electronics is essential for enhancing energy conversion and management systems, particularly in the context of sustainable energy solutions. This research proposal aims to investigate the design, optimization, and control strategies for achieving enhanced energy conversion and management through the seamless integration of motors and power electronics. The student would participate in Novel integration techniques, advanced control algorithms, and increased energy efficiency. The research will involve a combination of theoretical analysis, simulation studies, and practical experimentation. Extensive computer-based modelling and simulation will be conducted to evaluate the performance and efficiency of various integration techniques. Usecases will be created for testing and validating the proposed design and control strategies. This research will contribute to the development of efficient energy conversion and management systems. It has far-reaching applications in renewable energy, electric vehicles, and industrial automation, making it crucial for a sustainable future..
